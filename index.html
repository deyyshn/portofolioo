About Me

Hi! I'm Dermawati Siahaan, but you can simply call me Dee. I’m currently a student at Universitas Pertamina, majoring in Economics under the Faculty of Economics and Business. Beyond numbers and theories, I’m deeply passionate about the digital creative world, especially in areas like marketing, branding, content creation, and visual storytelling. Since childhood, I’ve always been fascinated by the way people communicate, connect, and respond to visuals and ideas. That curiosity has led me to explore how brands speak, how messages are crafted, and how digital platforms can shape perception. I enjoy combining strategic thinking with aesthetics, whether that’s through designing impactful visual content, understanding consumer behavior, or telling stories that resonate with the audience. I believe that creativity is not just about making things look good, but about making people feel something. Lately, I’ve been diving deeper into the intersection of design, marketing, and business — from how small local businesses (UMKM) grow their brand identities to how large corporations build emotional connections at scale.
